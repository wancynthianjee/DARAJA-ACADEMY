We are Daraja

Daraja is a highschool that is located in Nanyuki in Laikipia East near Doldol
Daraja was started in 2009 by Jason and Jenny Doherty who were History teavhers from America.
When Jason came to Africa and specifically Kenya for the fist time he had a dream of opening
a school that would help take girls that come from maginalized and humble communities to school that
was to help the girls go to learn.

His aim was to help those girls that wished to go to school to go so that they could learn and also
also help those girls who would run away from home due to some of the challenges that they were facing from
their communities such as forced marriages and circumcission.He also wanted to help those girls that were running
away from home violence.The street girls who used to live on the streets and wanted to get the opportunity to
learn and get an education
